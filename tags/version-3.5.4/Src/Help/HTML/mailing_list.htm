CodeSnip Mailing List

The CodeSnip mailing list is a low frequency mailing list. Subscribers receive email notifications of updates to the CodeSnip program and the Code Snippets online database.

You can subscribe to the mailing list when you register CodeSnip or by choosing the Tools | Join Mailing List menu option.

On subscribing to the mailing list you will be sent a welcome email. This email will advise how to confirm your membership of the list. Confirmation must be received within 14 days. Every email sent to the list contains information about how to unsubscribe.

Your email address will be used for no other purpose than to send CodeSnip update news, unless you have used it to subscribe to some other DelphiDabbler service.
