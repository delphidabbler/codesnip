CodeSnip News Dialog Box

This dialog box is displayed from the Help | CodeSnip News menu item.

It shows the latest news about CodeSnip and the online database. The news is obtained from CodeSnip's online RSS news feed. The titles of most items is clickable and will display a web page relevant to the news item when clicked. News items older than 92 days are not displayed.

If the news feed is not available or there is an error, a message to that effect is displayed.

You can display the RSS feed in your default web browser by clicking the RSS Feed button. Depending on your browser, you may be able to subscribe to the feed from there.
